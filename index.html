WHO WE ARE ?

Prosylab is an Egyptian company, established in 1994, We are a specialized company in the supply, installation, and maintenance of railway and metro spare parts in Egypt. With extensive industry experience, we support the development and sustainability of railway and urban transportation systems by delivering reliable, high-quality solutions. Our company is committed to providing products and services that meet international standards of safety, performance, and durability. We work closely with trusted manufacturers and technical partners to ensure that every component we supply contributes to efficient and uninterrupted operations.

Located in Cairo - Egypt, we serve clients across the Arab world, providing comprehensive support for all spare parts stock needs.

Prosylab

Prosylab provides high-quality assembly components and spare parts for locomotives, freight cars, and passenger coaches. we offer comprehensive solutions, including manufacturing, supply, troubleshooting, and repair services, tailored to the railway industry's needs.

  • Vision

    To become a leading and trusted partner in supplying high-quality railway and rolling stock spare parts, contributing to the development of safe, efficient, and sustainable rail transport systems worldwide.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to supply high-quality railway and train spare parts ,ensuring competitive pricing, timely delivery, and full compliance with technical and tender requirements.

  • Goals

    To become a leading and trusted partner in supplying high-quality railway and rolling stock systems worldwide.
